# Lazarus Report Global & Lazarus News

Independent Geopolitical Analysis & Global Security. Uncovering the buried truths behind global power shifts.

## 🏛️ Philosophy
The name **Lazarus** symbolizes rebirth and resurrection—specifically the **Resurrection of Information** (*Kebangkitan Informasi*). Our mission is to dig up and revive facts that have been buried beneath propaganda, censorship, and selective narratives. We provide a platform for independent, civilian-led intelligence and analysis, free from institutional affiliation.

## 🚀 Vision
To become a primary hub for global strategic intelligence, providing citizens with high-fidelity data and objective analysis regarding the multipolar world transition, military developments, and global financial realignments.

## 🛠️ Key Features
- **Global Intelligence Feed:** Real-time news aggregation covering geopolitics, military operations, global markets, and digital assets.
- **Multilingual Support:** Fully localized experience in English and Indonesian.
- **Conflict Tracker:** OSINT-driven dashboard for monitoring active global flashpoints and tactical developments.
- **Live AIS Ship Tracker:** Real-time maritime monitoring of strategic maritime routes, including the Persian Gulf and the Strait of Hormuz.
- **Live Flight Tracker:** Integrated air traffic monitoring for strategic and civilian aviation.
- **Market Signals:** Live ticker and analysis for Bitcoin, Ethereum, precious metals (Gold), and major financial indices.
- **Advanced News Engine:** Secure server-side proxying with intelligent deduplication across multiple global news sources to ensure a clean, relevant feed.
- **Premium UI/UX:** Built with a "Zen Minimalist Elegant" approach—featuring high-contrast dark mode, sharp typography, and lightning-fast performance.

## 🛡️ Security & Privacy
Lazarus Report Global is built with a focus on security:
- **Server-Side Security:** All third-party data fetching is handled via a secure proxy layer to protect sensitive configurations.
- **Rate Limiting:** Industrial-standard protection against scraping and automated abuse.
- **Hardened CSP:** Strict Content Security Policies to ensure safe embedding of real-time tracking tools.
